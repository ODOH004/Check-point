Appetizers

  • Meat Pie Recipe

    Meat Pie is one of the meat snacks recipes made with minced meat, potato and carrot filling.

  • Suya Recipe

    Suya is tender beef threaded on a skewer and baked or grilled to perfection in a spicy peanut sauce, the epitome of West African street food.

  • Chicken Shawarma Wrap with Garlic or Yogurt Sauce

    Chicken Shawarma wrap is so tender, smokey and flavorful. Marinated in spices and roasted to perfection. Wrapped in a warm flatbread and top with garlic sauce or yogurt sauce, and crispy lettuce.

Main Courses

  • Jollof rice

    Jollof rice is made from a base of rice, tomato, and broth that’s cooked down with bell peppers, habaneros (also called scotch bonnet peppers), curry, thyme, and bay leaves.

  • Egusi soup

    Egusi soup is a one-pot meal that packs in vegetables and protein. The main ingredient is egusi, which is ground or milled melon seeds..

  • Afang soup

    Afang is a soup made from the leaves of the afang plant, which is a climbing vine native to sub-Saharan Africa. It’s sometimes referred to as “wild spinach” in English. Afang soup differs depending on who’s cooking it, but this vegetable soup typically includes dried fish, crayfish, onions, and palm nut oil.

Desserts

  • Puff-Puff

    It is made by spicing batter, especially with diced fresh pepper, before deep-frying in vegetable oil.

  • Coconut Candy

    These are made from coconut fruit and are crunchy, chewable, and generally candy-like.

  • Chin Chin

    It is made from well-kneaded dough flavored with butter, milk, and eggs. Chin-chin is either deep-fried or oven-baked, and the size/shape is generally based on individual preference.
